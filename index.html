About Us

Opinions and feedback have become an integral part, especially for the business world. Be it to find out whether your followers prefer tea or coffee to find out which time is suitable for your employees to attend an all-hands meet, you’ll need polls.

Our express poll app lets you create smart online polls and helps launch them within minutes. With this poll app in place, you can save a lot of time

When you are done with creating a poll, you can embed it or share the poll link via email, social media etc.

With nothing more than a single click and collect real-time insights about the responses.
